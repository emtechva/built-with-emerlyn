  2. 02DesignMap the triggers, actions, conditions, data structure, integrations and exception paths.

  4. 04Launch and HandoverDeploy the workflow and provide documentation, usage instructions and maintenance recommendations.

Is this the right fit for your business?

This is a good fit when:

  • Your business receives regular leads, bookings or customer requests.
  • Your team repeats the same administrative steps every day.
  • Information is being copied between disconnected tools.
  • Follow-ups depend on staff remembering each task.
  • You already have a process that needs to become faster and more reliable.
  • You want documentation and a structured handover.

This may not be the right starting point when:

  • The process has not been defined or tested manually.
  • The request is a one-time personal task with no repeatable workflow.
  • The project requires a full custom software product rather than workflow automation.
  • Required platform access, sample data or process information cannot be provided.
  • Automation is expected to replace every human decision without oversight.
